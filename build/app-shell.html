Assumptions & sources

Recipes are the real ones from the datamined item list — including faction/artifact items (they need an artifact plus refined materials). Costs are broken all the way down to raw resources: the app recursively values each ingredient by crafting it from raw, and only buys the leaves it can't craft (raw resources, artifacts, tokens) at market price.

Market fees: Setup fee 2.5% applies only to sell orders; transaction tax is 8% (4% with Premium) on sale. Return rate reduces returnable material use per craft; artifacts never return.

Craftable count is how many you can make right now from the direct ingredients in your Inventory. Inventory & silver are manual by design — Albion exposes no inventory API, and the workarounds carry account risk.

Sources: item & recipe data from the community ao-bin-dumps; live prices & history from the Albion Online Data Project (crowdsourced — thin markets may be stale or empty); icons from Albion's official render service. Not affiliated with Sandbox Interactive.
