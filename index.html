Assumptions & sources

Market fees: Setup fee 2.5% applies only when placing a sell order; transaction tax is 8% (4% with Premium) and applies when the item sells. Instant Sell pays the current best buy-order price with tax only.

Return rate reduces effective material use per craft: qty × (1 − rate). Recipe quantities are estimates scaled from tier 4 — correct any of them in the ledger and the correction sticks.

Non-standard recipes (Avalonian, Royal, Keeper, Hell, Morgana, Undead, Fey, Crystal) show live market price and icon only; their true material lists must be checked in-game.

Materials & silver are manual by design. Albion exposes no public inventory API, and the workarounds carry account risk — this ledger will never attempt them.

Sources: prices & history from the community-run Albion Online Data Project (crowdsourced — thin markets may be stale or empty); item icons from Albion's official render service. Fonts from Google Fonts (optional; falls back to system fonts offline). Not affiliated with Sandbox Interactive.
